== Description ==

Payiyo Official Bitcoinn Payment Gateway for Wordpress(WooCommerce). Accept payments from your customers with bitcoin.


= Features of the plugin =

* 100% Free and Open Source
* No ID Required, No Bank Account Needed. Global, Anonymous, Secure, No Chargebacks, Zero Risk
* No Monthly Fee
* Accept Bitcoin payments Online on your Wordpress(WooCommerce) website.
* Direct Integration on your website, no external payment pages opens (as other payment gateways offer).
* Free Tech Support
* Automatic currency change. Show it in your own currency. Show your customer as BTC. (USD, EUR, TRY, GBP, BTC are accepted)
